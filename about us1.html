About Us

Welcome to Varshney’s Group of Restaurants — your digital companion in reconnecting with nature.

Brand Story: Varshney’s Group of Restaurants was founded with a simple but powerful idea: to bring people together through unforgettable food and warm hospitality. What began as a vision to create a welcoming dining destination has grown into a diverse hospitality brand offering multi-cuisine dining, premium desserts, elegant party halls, comfortable rooms, and reliable food delivery services. At Varshney’s, every dish tells a story. Our chefs combine traditional cooking techniques with modern culinary creativity to deliver flavors that satisfy every palate. From rich Indian delicacies and authentic Asian dishes to classic continental cuisine and handcrafted desserts, our menus are designed to create memorable dining experiences for families, friends, and celebrations of all kinds. Over time, Varshney’s expanded beyond a single restaurant concept into a complete hospitality ecosystem. Today, we provide beautifully designed event halls, private dining spaces, catering services, and a growing delivery network connecting customers with affiliated restaurants across major parts of India. Despite our growth, one thing has never changed — our commitment to quality, hospitality, and customer happiness. At Varshney’s, we believe every meal should feel special, every event should feel grand, and every guest should feel at home.

Founder Message: “When we started Varshney’s Group of Restaurants, our dream was not just to open a place to eat — but to build a destination where people could celebrate life’s most important moments. Food has a unique power. It brings families together, strengthens friendships, and creates memories that last forever. That belief has been the foundation of everything we do. At Varshney’s, we focus on three principles: quality, trust, and hospitality. Every recipe is prepared with care, every guest is treated with respect, and every experience is designed to exceed expectations. As we continue to grow our restaurants, event venues, and delivery network across India, our promise remains the same: to serve delicious food, provide exceptional service, and create spaces where people can gather, celebrate, and enjoy life.
Thank you for being part of the Varshney’s family.”
— Founder, Varshney’s Group of Restaurants

Our Vision: Our vision is to become one of India’s most trusted and recognized hospitality brands, known for exceptional dining experiences, innovative culinary offerings, and world-class event spaces. We aim to create a hospitality ecosystem where customers can enjoy premium food, memorable celebrations, and convenient delivery services under one trusted brand. By continuously expanding our restaurants, event halls, and partner network, we strive to make Varshney’s a destination that represents quality, celebration, and culinary excellence across India.

Our Mission: Our mission is to deliver outstanding hospitality experiences through: Exceptional Food Quality Preparing every dish using fresh ingredients, authentic recipes, and modern culinary techniques. Customer-First Service Ensuring every guest receives warm, attentive, and personalized service. Memorable Celebrations Providing beautifully designed halls and spaces for birthdays, weddings, corporate events, and family gatherings. Accessible Food Delivery Connecting customers with delicious meals through a reliable and expanding delivery network across India. Continuous Innovation Introducing new cuisines, experiences, and technology to enhance the way people dine, celebrate, and order food.

Restaurant Journey Timeline: 2018 – The Beginning The first Varshney’s restaurant opens with a focus on multi-cuisine dining and family hospitality. 2019 – Expanding the Menu Introduction of signature starters, premium desserts, and chef-curated specialty dishes. 2020 – Event Spaces Launch Opening of dedicated halls and rooms for birthday parties, weddings, and corporate functions. 2021 – Catering Services Varshney’s begins offering catering services for large events and celebrations. 2023 – Delivery Expansion Launch of door-to-door delivery services and partnerships with affiliated restaurants. 2025 – Growing Network Varshney’s expands its presence with new restaurants, event venues, and delivery coverage across major cities in India. Future Vision Expansion into nationwide restaurant partnerships, mobile ordering platforms, and premium hospitality experiences.

Achievements & Awards: Over the years, Varshney’s Group of Restaurants has earned recognition for its dedication to quality, service, and hospitality.
